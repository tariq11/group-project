The Miami Heat are an American professional basketball team based in Miami, Florida. The team is a member of the Southeast Division in the Eastern Conference of the National Basketball Association (NBA). They play their home games at the American Airlines Arena in Downtown Miami. The team owner is Micky Arison, The team president and de facto general manager is Pat Riley, and the head coach is Erik Spoelstra.

  • Formed in 1988 as one of the NBA's four expansion franchises
  • Won three league championships (in 2006, 2012 and 2013)
  • Won five conference titles and 11 division titles
  • The Heat won 27 games in a row, the second-longest streak in NBA history
